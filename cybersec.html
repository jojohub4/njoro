Vulnerability Assessment

Get your business network, systems, web applications scanned for any security vulnerabilities.

A vulnerability assessment is a systematic review of security weaknesses in information systems. It evaluates if the system is susceptible to any known vulnerabilities and recommends remediation and mitigation, if and whenever needed. Penetration testing takes it a step further by trying to legally exploit vulnerabilities found and assisting our customers patch the vulnerabilities.

Penetration Testing

We offer organizations and companies in Kenya penetration testing services to identify potential weak points against cyberattacks and to help them get ready for them.

System penetration testing is the process of finding weaknesses in programs and systems by purposefully using harsh techniques to assess how secure the network is—or is not—against the attacks. A penetration testing service's ultimate goal is to test the security flaws found during the scanning process and look into any possible penetration vectors.

cybersecurity Awareness--

Employees who receive security awareness training are more equipped to identify and reduce cyberthreats, which promotes a vigilant and resilient culture. It protects sensitive data and assets, lowers breaches, and minimizes human error.

cybersecurity awareness training is essential for businesses in Kenya. By educating employees on cybersecurity risks and best practices, businesses can reduce the risk of cyber attacks, comply with industry regulations, and protect their sensitive information from unauthorized access.
